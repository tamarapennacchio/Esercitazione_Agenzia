Tim Lahan

timlahan.com

San Francisco-based artist Tim Lahan’s naughty, witty artwork is the product of his ability to digest and illustrate clever glitches, concepts and interesting visual cues from the world around him. “I generally aim to make things as simple as possible,” says Tim.
“Sometimes they come out funny.” The power of his light and carefree language lends itself very well to any type of representation and becomes a distinctive feature of his aesthetic, which is never boring, but is constantly renewed with cheerful experimentation.
