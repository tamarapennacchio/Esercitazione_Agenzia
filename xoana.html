Xoana Herrera

xoanaherrera.com

The portfolio of LA-based illustrator Xoana Herrera is an explosion of sumptuous, stylish character scenes that looks ideal. Xoana trained as a graphic designer in Argentina but over time found herself drawn to the world of illustration after a five-year stint at Buck agency.
Now, alongside founding a feminist creative collective where she creates graceful, complex and accessible imagery which would be as at home in an ad campaign as it would be in a children’s book. “As an artist I think it is up to us to keep evolving and taking creative decisions,” she says.
