Karlotta Freier

karlottafreier.com

Karlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist with a background in illustration. She graduated from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts. In addition to her creative she also dedicates time to sharing her knowledge.
She has taught illustration at the China Academy of Art and spoken as a guest speaker at universities across the United States. Freier's work has also been exhibited in a variety of solo and group exhibitions, as well as festivals worldwide. Several times her work has been awarded.
